## General Guidelines for All Videos:

### Color Palette:
- Primary: Black (#000000), Dark Grey (#0A0A0A, #1A1A1A)
- Accents: Gold/Amber (#BF953F, #FCF6BA), White (#FFFFFF)
- Optional: Cyan (#00FFFF) for tech elements

### Motion Style:
- Slow, smooth, elegant movements
- No fast cuts or jarring transitions
- Cinematic camera work
- Professional and refined

### Aesthetic Keywords:
- Luxury, minimalist, high-end, sophisticated
- Dark, moody, dramatic lighting
- Clean, precise, architectural
- Futuristic, high-tech, premium

### Technical Requirements:
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD) minimum
- Frame Rate: 30fps or 60fps
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- Duration: 10-15 seconds
- **Must be seamlessly loopable** (start and end frames should match)
